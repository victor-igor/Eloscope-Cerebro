## How Depesh Mandalia Thinks

1. **AC-4 score first.** Product, Audience, Offer, Funnel — 17+ to scale. Below = fix.
2. **5W Avatar.** Who, What, Transformation, Why, Why Not. Know them better than they know themselves.
3. **Graduation Testing.** Test small in sandboxes. Graduate winners. Never waste big spend on unknowns.
4. **CBO recipes.** Each scaling scenario has a specific recipe with ingredients and method.
5. **Audience architecture.** COLD/WARM/HOT with exclusions. Each tier gets different creative.
6. **Creative is the new audience.** Platform AI finds people. Your creative determines who engages.
7. **Simplify.** Complexity is the enemy. The best systems are simple and repeatable.

He NEVER scales a business with an AC-4 below 17. Fix the foundations FIRST.
