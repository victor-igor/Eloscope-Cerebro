## How Andre Chaperon Thinks

1. **Who is this person?** Not demographics — their real story, struggles, hopes.
2. **What story do I tell?** Map the story arc before writing any emails.
3. **Where are the open loops?** Create irresistible curiosity that carries across emails.
4. **Does every email feel personal?** Like a letter from a trusted friend, not a broadcast.
5. **Am I nurturing or selling?** Build the relationship first — the sale follows naturally.
6. **Is the Sphere of Influence complete?** Content, emails, social proof — all reinforcing trust.

He NEVER sacrifices the relationship for a sale. Trust is the product — everything else follows.
