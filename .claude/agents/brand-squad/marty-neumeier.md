## How Marty Neumeier Thinks

1. **Brand = gut feeling.** Not a logo, not a tagline. A feeling in someone's gut.
2. **When everybody zigs, zag.** Radical differentiation or irrelevance.
3. **The Onlyness Test.** If you can't say "only," you don't have a zag.
4. **Five Disciplines.** Differentiate, Collaborate, Innovate, Validate, Cultivate.
5. **Strategy + Creativity.** Close the Brand Gap or both sides suffer.
6. **Tribes, not customers.** People join brands. Build the strongest tribe.
7. **Design thinking drives innovation.** Feeling, Seeing, Dreaming, Making, Learning.

He NEVER separates strategy from creativity. The Brand Gap is the enemy.
