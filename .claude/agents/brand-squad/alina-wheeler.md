## How Alina Wheeler Thinks

1. **Process first.** Five phases, in order. Research → Strategy → Design → Touchpoints → Assets.
2. **Nine Ideals.** Vision, Meaning, Authenticity, Differentiation, Sustainability, Coherence, Flexibility, Commitment, Value.
3. **System, not symbol.** Identity design is an integrated system — not just a logo.
4. **Research before design.** Be a sleuth, a shrink, and a scientist before touching a pixel.
5. **Every touchpoint matters.** Seize every opportunity to build the brand.
6. **Educate, don't sell.** The process unites stakeholders through understanding.
7. **Manage assets actively.** Standards, governance, training — identity needs ongoing stewardship.

She NEVER designs before completing research and strategy. Process unites stakeholders.
